Help us launch ChildBloom 🌱

We're one step from the Google Play Store — and we just need a few kind people to test the app. It takes about two minutes and three taps. Thank you for being one of them. 💚

Open this on your Android phone

The app installs from the Google Play Store, so finish these steps on the phone you'll use it on. Scan the code — or copy the link.

Step 0 of 3 0%
1

Join the testers group

Google only lets people who are in our testers group download the app. Joining takes one tap.

Join the group

On the Google Groups page, tap “Join group”. Use the same Google account you'll use on the Play Store.

2

Become a tester

This tells Google you're in. Tap the button, then tap “Become a tester” on the page that opens.

Opt in as a tester

You should see ”You are a tester.” Use the same Google account as Step 1, and make sure your Play Store country is India.
If you just joined the group, wait 2 minutes first.

3

Install ChildBloom

Open the Play Store listing and tap Install. That's it — you're officially a tester. 🎉

Open on Google Play

Just opted in? Google can take up to ~15 minutes to unlock the download. If it says “item not found,” wait a bit and retry.

🎉

You're a ChildBloom tester!

Thank you — genuinely. Keep the app installed for the next two weeks so it counts. That's all we need from you. 💚

It says “URL not found” on the opt-in page

This error means Google doesn't recognise your account as an approved tester. Three things to check:

1. Wait 2 minutes after joining the group. Google Groups membership takes a moment to propagate to Play Store.

2. Use the same Google account everywhere. The Gmail you joined the group with must be the active account in your Play Store (Play Store → profile icon → check the account at the top).

3. Your Play Store country must be India. The app is currently published for India only. If your Play Store account is set to another country, opt-in won't work yet.

Still stuck? Email childbloomenterprise@gmail.com and we'll sort it out.

It says “item not found” or “app not available”

Wait ~15 minutes after opting in. Google needs a little time to unlock the download for your account.

Use the same Google account everywhere — the one that joined the group and opted in must be the active account in your Play Store (Play Store → profile icon → check the account at the top).

It's Android only for now. iPhone can't install the test build, but you can use ChildBloom free in any browser at childbloom.in.

Still stuck? Email childbloomenterprise@gmail.com and we'll sort it out.
